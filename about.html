The Work

At the intersection of leadership, psychology, and performance under pressure.

Dr. Lizzie Bronte is a leadership advisor and founder of Bronte Advisory, where she helps high-performing professional women step into their authority in high-stakes environments — without over-preparing, second-guessing, or shrinking.

With a Ph.D. in Global Leadership and Organizational Management and coaching certifications informed by research on imposter syndrome, her work sits at the intersection of leadership, psychology, and performance under pressure.

Dr. Bronte specialises in supporting accomplished women who are externally successful but internally navigating hesitation, visibility pressure, and self-doubt as their influence grows. Through her proprietary VOICE Framework™, she equips clients to communicate with clarity, make decisive choices, and lead with grounded confidence.

"This space is for women ready to lead with authority, conviction, and composure — wherever their influence takes them."

— Dr. Lizzie Bronte

Her approach blends research, Stoic philosophy, and lived experience to help women move from self-editing to self-trust — transforming how they show up in meetings, decision-making moments, and leadership spaces.

The Methodology

The VOICE
Framework™

A proprietary framework built from research, coaching practice, and deep study of how high-performing women close the gap between external achievement and internal authority.

V

Visibility

Owning your presence in high-stakes rooms without shrinking, over-explaining, or performing.

O

Ownership

Taking authority over your decisions, your narrative, and your influence without waiting for permission.

I

Intuition

Trusting your instincts under pressure — not overriding them with external validation or excessive preparation.

C

Conviction

Communicating with clarity and certainty — even when others push back, question, or challenge your perspective.

E

Embodiment

Leading from a place of grounded identity — so authority becomes natural, not a role you have to step into.

The Approach

Research, philosophy,
and lived experience

Dr. Bronte's approach is not a collection of confidence hacks or motivational frameworks. It is a rigorous, research-grounded methodology that helps women understand the psychology behind self-doubt — and systematically dismantle it.

Drawing on Stoic philosophy, organizational psychology, and over a decade of working at the intersection of leadership and identity, she helps clients move from self-editing to self-trust in a way that is lasting — not situational.

The result is a leader who doesn't manage her confidence. She simply leads.

Research-Grounded

Every framework and intervention is rooted in peer-reviewed research on imposter syndrome, leadership psychology, and organizational behaviour — not anecdote or trend.

Stoic Philosophy

The ancient discipline of separating what is within your control from what is not is quietly revolutionary for women navigating modern high-stakes leadership.

Lived Experience

Dr. Bronte brings her own experience of navigating authority, visibility, and identity to every client engagement — with earned empathy and hard-won insight.
